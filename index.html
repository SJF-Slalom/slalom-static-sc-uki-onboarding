A welcome from your MD

Welcome aboard
Sonali
Sonali Fenner
Managing Director, UK&I Strategy & Consulting

Whether you’re joining us for the first time or returning after time away, I’m genuinely glad you’re here. You’re joining a team that plays a unique and vital role in how Slalom operates. Strategy & Consulting (S&C) is where Client relationships are shaped, where big questions get asked and where the vision for transformative work begins. We are, in the truest sense, the front door to Slalom’s value.

Our mission is simple: we are Vision to Value. We help our clients dream bigger from the very first conversation, bringing together expertise in Business and Technology Strategy, Customer Experience, and Organisational Transformation to deliver outcomes that genuinely shift the needle. When we do that well, we don’t just deliver brilliant consulting — we create the conditions for Slalom’s broader capabilities in data & AI, engineering, cloud, enterprise technology, delivery and beyond to do their best work too. S&C is both the spark and the strategic thread that runs through the engagements our Clients value most.

You’re joining at an exciting moment. We have recently brought together our UK and Ireland S&C teams into a single, unified UK&I structure. This is more than an organisational change; it’s a deliberate investment in building a stronger, more connected consulting team. By combining the depth of experience, client relationships, and market knowledge across both the UK and Ireland, we’ve created a team with greater scale, broader reach, and a richer diversity of perspective. For you, that means more people to learn from, more Client contexts to work across, and a bigger platform from which to grow your career.

But UK&I is just one part of the picture. Our Strategy, CX, and Transformation Capabilities each have thriving global communities of practice that span the Americas, EMEA, and APAC. That means the thinking, the frameworks, the propositions and the Client stories you’ll encounter here aren’t developed in isolation. You’ll have the opportunity to collaborate with S&C colleagues internationally, to leverage global assets and research in your day-to-day work, and to contribute your own expertise back into that network. Very few firms of our size can offer that combination of local intimacy and global reach and it’s something I’d encourage you to lean into from day one.

Our culture matters as much as our capabilities. At Slalom, we believe we do our best work when we feel connected as people, not just as colleagues. We are client-forward and high-touch. We approach every challenge with a “how might we” mindset rather than a “why we can’t” one. And we genuinely have each other’s backs — across Capabilities, across geographies, and across levels of seniority. You’ll feel that from the start, and I hope you’ll help us keep building it.

In this pack, you’ll find everything you need to get oriented around S&C: who we are, how we’re structured, the leadership team, our ways of working, and the strategic context that shapes what we do. Take the time to absorb it, and please don’t hesitate to ask questions — there are no silly ones, and everyone here remembers what it’s like to be new. Welcome aboard!
